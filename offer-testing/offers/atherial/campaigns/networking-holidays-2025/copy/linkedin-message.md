## Personalization Variables

- `{{firstname}}` - Contact's first name from CSV

---

## Message Breakdown

### Line 1: Holiday Greeting + Question
**"Happy holidays {{firstname}}! What's in store for you in 2026?"**

- **Purpose:** Warm, personal greeting that invites response
- **Tone:** Friendly, genuine interest
- **Personalization:** First name
- **Open-ended question:** Encourages them to share their plans

### Line 2-3: Service Introduction + Referral Ask
**"Let me know if I can be a resource to anyone in your network who wants to implement custom AI agents or internal tools."**

- **Purpose:** Explain offering without hard pitch
- **Approach:** Referral ask (easier than direct sale)
- **Positioning:** Helper/resource, not salesperson

### Line 4: Proof + CTA
**"Here are demo videos of some of what we've shipped to production: https://www.atherial.ai/work. Happy to share lessons and best practices."**

- **Proof:** Portfolio link shows credibility
- **CTA:** Offer to share knowledge (soft, valuable)
- **Production emphasis:** Real systems, not prototypes

---

## Why This Message Works

✅ **Personal but scalable** - First name personalization, rest is template  
✅ **Holiday timing** - Natural reason to reach out  
✅ **Question invites response** - Not just broadcasting  
✅ **Soft ask** - Referrals easier than direct pitch  
✅ **Proof included** - Portfolio link for credibility  
✅ **No pressure** - Offer to help, not sell  
✅ **Clear value prop** - What we do in plain language  

---

## Expected Response Rate

**Benchmark:** 10-20% reply rate (warm audience)

**Types of responses:**
1. **Engaged:** Tell you about their 2026 plans, ask questions
2. **Interested:** Want to learn more about services
3. **Referral:** Know someone who needs this
4. **Polite decline:** Thanks but not relevant
5. **No response:** Most common

---

## Follow-Up Strategy

**For Engaged/Interested replies:**
- Continue conversation naturally
- Ask about their specific AI needs
- Offer to hop on a call

**For Referral replies:**
- Thank them
- Ask for intro or permission to mention their name
- Follow up with referred contact

**For No Response:**
- No follow-up (this is networking, not sales)
- They got the message, ball is in their court
