## Quick Summary

**What we do:**  
Build custom AI roleplay apps for B2B companies where their sales reps can practice unlimited calls with voice AI trained to act like their actual target prospects, then receive coaching from AI trained on their winning sales playbooks.

**Who it's for:**  
Sales leaders (CRO, VP Sales, Director of Sales) at B2B companies under 100 employees in the US who need to ramp new sales reps faster.

**Key problem we solve:**  
Sales reps aren't getting enough quality practice before real customer calls, leading to long ramp times (3-6 months), lost deals, and wasted manager time on repetitive coaching.

**Our differentiator:**  
Custom-built service (not off-the-shelf product) with AI trained on THEIR specific buyers and playbooks, delivered as voice (not text) to mimic real calls.
