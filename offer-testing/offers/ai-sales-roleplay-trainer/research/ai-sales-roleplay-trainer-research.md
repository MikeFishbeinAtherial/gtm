---

## Market Insights

### Trends
- Sales leaders increasingly frustrated with long ramp times
- Remote work = harder to shadow/coach reps organically
- AI adoption in sales accelerating (Gong, ChatGPT, etc.)
- Companies want personalization, not generic tools

### Key Stats
- Average sales rep ramp time: 3-6 months to full productivity
- 57% of sales leaders say coaching is their #1 time drain
- Companies spend $10K-$50K per rep on training/ramp

---

## Buyer Research

### Pain Points by Role

**CRO/VP Sales:**
- Pressure to hit revenue targets with underperforming reps
- Not enough time to coach everyone
- High cost of hiring/churning reps

**Director of Sales:**
- Managing mix of experienced + new reps
- Repetitive coaching on basics
- Inconsistent performance across team

**Founder/CEO (small teams):**
- Every rep matters - can't afford long ramp
- Limited sales leadership bandwidth
- Need scalable training solution
