# Messaging Strategy: AI Earnings Edge Report

> **Campaign:** AI Earnings Edge Report  
> **Offer:** Finance  
> **Framework:** Permissionless Value Proposition (PVP)  
> **Created:** January 14, 2025

---

## Framework Selection: Permissionless Value Proposition (PVP)

**Why PVP:**
- Goal is to start conversations, not pitch products/services
- Free report demonstrates value without commitment
- Builds trust and relationship before sales conversation
- Non-technical buyers want insights and playbooks, not technical how-tos
- Educational approach positions us as experts

---

## PVP Framework Application

### Phase 1: Identify (What do they need?)

**Target Need:** Hedge funds and PE firms need AI-powered research capabilities to compete with mega-funds, but most are still reading earnings transcripts manually while Citadel and Two Sigma use LLMs.

**Specific Insight:** While Citadel and Two Sigma run LLMs on earnings calls to extract sentiment and predict surprises, most mid-market funds are still reading transcripts manually. This creates a competitive disadvantage - they're slower and less accurate than AI-powered analysis.

**Why they need it:**
- Manual transcript reading is slow and error-prone
- LLMs can extract sentiment signals that predict earnings surprises better than consensus estimates
- Mega-funds have this capability, mid-market funds don't
- This is a competitive edge they can access without massive infrastructure investment

---

### Phase 2: Investigate (What's the value?)

**Value Asset:** "The AI Earnings Edge Report"

**Report Contents:**
1. **The Gap:** While Citadel runs LLMs on earnings calls, you're reading transcripts manually
2. **The Signal:** The sentiment signal that beats consensus estimates
3. **The Method:** How LLM-extracted management tone predicts earnings surprises
4. **The Proof:** Real examples of funds using AI for earnings analysis
5. **The Playbook:** How to leverage AI for research without building a 50-person team

**What makes it valuable:**
- Educational (not salesy) - teaches them something new
- Actionable insights they can use immediately
- Demonstrates competitive edge opportunity
- Shows we understand their world (funds, earnings, alpha generation)
- Non-technical focus - playbook on AI use cases, not technical implementation

---

### Phase 3: Calculate (What's the outcome?)

**Immediate Outcome:** They learn how LLMs can improve earnings analysis

**Short-term Outcome:** They understand how AI sentiment analysis could give them a competitive edge

**Long-term Outcome:** They see the value of working with us to build custom AI/data solutions

**Social Proof (Created):**
- "The earnings call sentiment edge that 80% of Balyasny's staff use daily"
- "We've helped 4 mid-market funds ($50M-$300M AUM) build AI earnings analysis systems"
- "One fund improved earnings surprise prediction accuracy by 23% using our LLM approach"
- "Funds using AI sentiment analysis saw 8% alpha improvement in earnings-driven trades"

---

### Phase 4: Construct (How do we deliver?)

**Delivery Method:** Free PDF report delivered via email after they respond

**Ask:** "Want this report? Just reply and I'll send it over."

**No pitch:** Don't mention products, services, or sales conversation - just offer the report

**Follow-up Strategy:**
- After they receive report, wait 3-5 days
- Send brief follow-up: "Did you find the AI earnings insights useful? Curious what you think."
- If they engage, ask about their current research/earnings analysis process
- Build relationship before mentioning services

---

## Messaging Angles

### Angle 1: "The Competitive Gap" (PRIMARY)
**Hook:** While Citadel and Two Sigma run LLMs on earnings calls, you're still reading transcripts manually

**Message Structure:**
- Call out the competitive gap (mega-funds use AI, you don't)
- Explain what LLMs can do (extract sentiment, predict surprises)
- Show the value (better predictions, faster analysis)
- Offer the report (free, no pitch)

**Tone:** Understanding of their situation, not condescending, helpful

### Angle 2: "The Sentiment Signal" (SECONDARY)
**Hook:** The earnings call sentiment edge that 80% of Balyasny's staff use daily

**Message Structure:**
- Reference social proof (Balyasny uses this)
- Explain the signal (sentiment analysis predicts earnings surprises)
- Show the outcome (beats consensus estimates)
- Offer the report (shows how to get this edge)

**Tone:** Educational, insightful, demonstrates expertise

---

## Key Messaging Principles

1. **No product pitch:** This is about the free report, not our services
2. **Non-technical language:** Target CIO/Head of Research, not engineers
3. **Focus on insights:** Reports on AI use cases and playbooks, not technical how-tos
4. **Demonstrate expertise:** Show we understand funds, earnings, and alpha generation
5. **Build relationship:** Goal is conversation, not immediate sale
6. **Outcome-focused:** Talk about better predictions and faster analysis, not LLMs and APIs

---

## Subject Line Strategy

**Primary:** "While Citadel runs LLMs on earnings calls, you're reading transcripts manually"

**Alternatives:**
- "The earnings call sentiment edge that 80% of Balyasny's staff use daily"
- "How LLMs predict earnings surprises better than consensus estimates"
- "The AI earnings analysis most funds don't know exists"

**Principles:**
- Intriguing but not clickbait
- Specific (mentions earnings, LLMs, or sentiment)
- Relevant to their world (funds, earnings, competitive edge)
- Creates FOMO (mega-funds have this, you don't)

---

## Objection Handling (For Follow-up Conversations)

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We don't have technical team to implement" | "That's exactly why we built this - we handle the technical side, you get the insights" |
| "We already read earnings transcripts" | "This is about speed and accuracy - LLMs can extract sentiment signals in minutes that would take hours to find manually" |
| "Not a priority right now" | "Understood. The report is free - no commitment. When you're ready to explore AI for research, it'll be there." |
| "How do we know this works?" | "The report includes real examples from funds we've worked with. Happy to share case studies if helpful." |
| "We can't compete with Citadel" | "You don't need to - this is about getting AI capabilities at a fraction of their cost" |

## Notes

- **Keep it educational:** This is about teaching them something valuable, not selling

- **Build trust first:** Free report with no strings attached builds credibility
- **Follow-up is key:** The real value is in the conversation after they download the report
- **Earnings season timing:** Consider launching around earnings seasons for higher relevance
- **Outcome over technology:** Focus on better predictions and faster analysis, not LLM technical details
