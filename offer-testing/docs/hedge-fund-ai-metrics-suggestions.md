## What Hedge Funds Actually Compare

1. **vs. Consensus Estimates:** Did AI beat sell-side consensus?
2. **vs. Manual Analysis:** Is AI better/faster than their current process?
3. **vs. Other Funds:** Are they falling behind competitors using AI?
4. **vs. Benchmarks:** Did AI-driven decisions outperform the market?
5. **vs. Cost:** Is the ROI worth the investment?

## Key Insight

Hedge funds care about **relative performance** - not just absolute metrics. They want to know:
- Are they beating consensus?
- Are they faster than competitors?
- Are they catching signals others miss?
- Are they generating alpha?

The "client of ours" addition helps because it:
- Adds credibility (real client, not hypothetical)
